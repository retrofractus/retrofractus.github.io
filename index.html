01Mission
Most of the largest sources of animal suffering remain under-researched relative to their scale. Vale Research exists to close that gap — building tight, well-resourced programs that pair domain experts with new researchers, set clear deliverables, and conclude on a defined timeline.

Developing welfare indicators that can be used in the wild, where conventional captive-animal methods break down.

Vaccinating wildlife at scale

Investigating the operational and biological pathways to delivering vaccines to wild animal populations at meaningful scale.

Estimating wild insect populations

Developing technology that can reliably estimate insect population sizes in the wild — a prerequisite for almost any quantitative work on insect welfare.

03How we work

Research with deadlines.

/ 01

Scoped, not open-ended

Every program starts with a written specification: what counts as success, what gets published, and when. No drift, no perpetual investigations.

/ 02

Mentorship-dense

Each cohort pairs early-career researchers with senior advisors from academia and industry. We measure mentor time in hours per fellow per week, not titles.

/ 03

Publish or retract

Findings — including null and inconvenient results — are released on schedule. We commit to the deliverable before the data comes in.
